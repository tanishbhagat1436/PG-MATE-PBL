About PG Mate

Connecting PG owners and tenants for a better living experience.

Our Mission

Finding the right Paying Guest accommodation can be stressful, and managing one can be even more chaotic. PG Mate was born from a simple idea: to digitalize and simplify the entire PG ecosystem. Our mission is to create a transparent, efficient, and hassle-free environment for both property owners and residents.

Who We Are

We are a team of tech enthusiasts and former students who have experienced the challenges of PG life firsthand. We're dedicated to building a platform that solves real-world problems with smart, user-friendly technology, making life easier for everyone involved.
