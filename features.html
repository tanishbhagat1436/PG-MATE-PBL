Our Core Features

Everything you and your PG need, all in one place.

Seamless Rent Payments

Tenants can pay their monthly rent directly through our app using UPI, cards, or net banking. Owners receive payments instantly and can track all transactions in a centralized dashboard. No more cash hassles or missed payments!

Effortless Resident Management

Owners can manage tenant profiles, track move-in/move-out dates, and handle documentation digitally. Our platform simplifies onboarding and maintains a complete record of all residents, past and present.

Instant Maintenance Requests

Leaky faucet? Wi-Fi down? Tenants can raise maintenance tickets directly from the app with photos. Owners get notified instantly, can assign the task, and track its status until closure, ensuring happy residents.
